Gina Valvo is a motion designer | Kristen Mickulesku is an analog photographer | Together we are Deliberate Living Co | Based in Denver, Colorado | We live to travel

Deliberate Living Co is located at the intersection of movement and stillness. As a design, animation, & film photography team, we believe opposites attract and blend to form something unique and unexpected. Our style is complex and clear, quiet and clever. We are observers and problem solvers that enjoy the challenge of taking your ideas to the next level.

film photography

Film requires a purposeful approach and provides a higher quality image and incredible vibrancy, color, and texture. It reminds us to slow down and embrace the most beautiful things in life. We would love to share our passion with you.

Whatever the occasion, or none at all, we will work with you to craft the perfect shoot. We collaborate with you to express your vision and personality and we offer our unique perspective on location, styling, and overall good vibes.

motion & illustration

Animation is a powerful medium that can evoke emotion and bring depth to your brand. We have created custom illustration and animation for events, social media, web, and broadcast. If you have ideas for a design or motion graphics project, big or small, we would love to chat.

print shop

We released our first print collection in April 2016. Themed "Home", these photos resonate with us on what it means to have a sense of home; a place to connect and recharge. We selected five photos, shot in Colorado on 35mm film and printed locally using archival ink on sustainable bamboo paper. We hope you enjoy these photos for a lifetime and beyond.

Check out the shop for wall-sized prints, postcard packs, and stickers! We are also accepting custom print orders at this time, so get in touch!
